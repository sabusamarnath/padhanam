# Bet

The strategic intent. Read at phase audits, not every session.

## What we are building

An agentic workflow SaaS platform. Core differentiator is the observability and optimization layer that production agentic systems need but most teams do not build because it is hard. NotebookLM-plus-Gems agent surface sits on top.

## Why this differentiates

Most teams using agents either ship undifferentiated wrappers around frontier models or burn senior engineering time building observability themselves. A platform that ships production-grade trace capture, evaluation harness, and active testing as default eliminates that work for users.

## Personal goal for the operator

Phase 1 is a learning sprint. By the end, fluency in building and assessing prompts and agents on a stack optimized for LLM use. Phase 2 pivots based on what Phase 1 surfaces.

## What success looks like at end of Phase 1

- A single tenant runs locally with the full stack
- One agent can be configured, run, audited, and optimized through the platform's own tooling
- The evaluation harness produces meaningful quality signals
- The trace capture layer surfaces optimization recommendations, not just data
- The operator can explain every architectural decision and why it was made
